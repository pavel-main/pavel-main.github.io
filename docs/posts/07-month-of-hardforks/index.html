The Month of Hard Forks

Oct 11, 2022 · 🇬🇧

Mid September to mid October of 2022 was pretty intense - several cryptocurrencies have performed major protocol upgrades.

Full list:

  • 🗓️ 15 Sep - Ethereum, The Merge
  • 🗓️ 20 Sep - Decentralized Social, Social Layer Hard Fork
  • 🗓️ 22 Sep - Cardano, Vasil upgrade
  • 🗓️ 28 Sep - Theta Network, v3.4.0 upgrade
  • 🗓️ 06 Oct - Binance Smart Chain, v1.1.15 hot fix and hard-fork release
  • 🗓️ 12 Oct - Binance Smart Chain, v1.1.16 Moran hardfork upgrade
