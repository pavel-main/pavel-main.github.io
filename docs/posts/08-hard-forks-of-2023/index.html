Hard Forks of 2023

Jan 22, 2023 · 🇬🇧

Following intense hard fork avalanche in late 2022, there will be at least 5 planned protocol upgrades coming this year.

List of upgrades

  1. 🗓️ 17 Jan 2023 - Polygon, Delhi Hardfork
  2. 🗓️ 31 Jan 2023 - Tron, Aristotle1
  3. 🗓️ 06 Feb 2023 - Decentralized Social, Associations and Access Groups fork
  4. 🗓️ 13 Feb 2023 - NEAR, Protocol v582
  5. 🗓️ 08 Mar 2023 - Decentralized Social, Associations spending limits improvements
  6. 🗓️ 20 Mar 2023 - NEAR, Meta Transactions
  7. 🗓️ 20 Mar 2023 - Blockstack, Stacks 2.1
  8. 🗓️ 29 Mar 2023 - Tezos, Mumbai
  9. 🗓️ 12 Apr 2023 - Ethereum, Shapella
  10. 🗓️ ?? Apr 2023 - Optimism, Bedrock
  11. 🗓️ 15 May 2023 - Bitcoin Cash, 2023-05-15 Network Upgrade

Footnotes

  1. Tron developers made an announcement in their Discord community 3 days before the forced upgrade, stating:

    Please complete your deployment to the new version before 23:59 January 30, 2023 (SGT). Otherwise, it will affect the node synchronization.

  2. From NEAR changelog for version 1.3.10:

    An empty protocol upgrade to facilitate migration of the network interfaces in the next version.

    Voting for upgrading to protocol version 58 will start on 2023-02-13 15:00:00 UTC
